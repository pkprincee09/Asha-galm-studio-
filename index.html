Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know.

How do I book a bridal appointment?
You can use the booking form below, call us at +91 92780 82123, or message us on WhatsApp. For bridal bookings, we recommend reserving 2–3 months in advance to secure your preferred date.
What products do you use for makeup?
We use only premium brands — MAC, Huda Beauty, Bobbi Brown, L'Oréal Professionnel, and Inglot. All products are dermatologically tested and skin-friendly.
Do you offer home services for bridal makeup?
Yes, we offer doorstep bridal services across Kushinagar, Deoria, Gorakhpur and nearby cities. Travel charges apply based on distance.
What are your current special offers?
We're offering Stylish Haircut at ₹199, Hair Smoothening at ₹2,999, Hydra Facial at ₹1,999, Manicure & Pedicure at ₹499, and a Combo Pack (De-Tan, Cleanup, Threading, Upper Lip) at ₹499 — among many others. Check our offers section above for the full list.
Is the salon safe and hygienic?
Absolutely. We follow strict sanitisation protocols — all tools are sterilised after every use, single-use disposables for waxing, and clean linens for every client.
